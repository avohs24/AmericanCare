Our Services

General Medicine

Our professionals are able to diagnose and treat many common conditions via telemedicine. This saves you a trip to the doctor's office, while also enabling our providers to treat as many patients as possible.

Psychiatric Services

Our psychiatric services enable qualified specialists to give treatment to patients remotely, while also growing national access to behavioral health services.

Remote Patient Monitoring

A digital connection between our observation center and a patient's home is created in order to transmit medical data instantly. This provides the patient with state-of-the-art monitoring from the comfort of their home.


About Us

Our company was founded in 2014, when we realized that technology could be used to revolutionize the health care industry. Our vision was to make medical care more affordable and convenient for patients. Today we employ hundreds of doctors from over 10 different specialties in order to provide our patients with only the best medical advice. Our priority is to offer patients faster, cheaper, and more reliable health care, through secure video chats in the comfort of their home.
